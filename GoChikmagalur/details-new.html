Dubai Aquarium And Underwater Zoo

Visit the Dubai Aquarium and Underwater Zoo to experience sea life and life of sea creatures in a natural and controlled environment but no less in magnificence. A tour of the underwater life is perfect for your family time.

Key Highlights

Enjoy the ultimate underwater experience in the heart of Downtown Dubai at the Dubai Aquarium.
The aquarium holds approximately 10 million liters of seawater. Perfect to learn about 300 species of marine creatures like sharks, piranha, their ecosystems and habitat.
The aquarium tunnel is 48-m in length which is the base of the aquarium.
You can choose from our Standard, Explorer, and VIP pass that offer various benefits depending on the pass you choose. While booking the ticket you can choose whether you want to go for transport or not.
